# S02: Provider recovery actions

**Goal:** Make provider recovery actions explicit, safe, and routed through AppModel/service boundaries.
**Demo:** A user can retry, reconnect, repair, or clear provider credentials from consistent provider-aware actions.

## Must-Haves

- Reconnect, repair, clear, and retry behaviors are provider-aware and tested.
- Credential material remains behind repository/service boundaries.
- User-facing errors identify the provider and next action without secret exposure.

## Proof Level

- This slice proves: integration

## Integration Closure

AppModel recovery methods coordinate SessionKeyImportService, ChatGPTSessionRepository, UsageService, and ChatGPTUsageService safely.
